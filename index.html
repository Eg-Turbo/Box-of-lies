Box Of Lies Generator

This is a Box Of Lies generator (instructions open in new tab), designed for playing the EFL / ESL game of the same name. A random picture is generated, and students can choose to describe it or invent their own description. The game was made famous by the Tonight Show with Jimmy Fallon, in which the host and a celebrity describe/invent strange objects.
